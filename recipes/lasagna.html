lasagna

Lasagna is Italy's gift to the world

Ingredients

    1 kg of ground lean beef
    2 can's of canned tomatoes
    1 cup onion
    olive oil
    1 can of crushed tomatoes
    2 gloves garlic
    1/2 cup of water
    1 tbs sugar
    4 tbs chopped parsley
    1 tbs dried basil
    1 teaspoon salt (salt too taste)
    1 teaspoon fennel seeds
    1 teaspoon black pepper
    12 lasagna noodle
    450g ricotta cheese
    1 egg
    100g mozzarella
    100g parmesan cheese

Directions

    Step 1

Gather all the Ingredients

    step 2

cook the ground beef,onion,garlic in medium heat until brown

    step 3

add the tomatoes,water,seasoning and let it simmer for about one and a half h

    step 4

Boil lasagna noodle in salted water 8'-10' minutes.Drain noodles and rinse with cold water

    step5

bake
